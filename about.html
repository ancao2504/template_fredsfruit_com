    OUR STORY

    Family owned

    Founding Owner and Director of the business Fred Pisciuneri migrated to Australia from Italy in 1956. Fred started work in a Bankstown fruit market at the age of 14 where heavy lifting was the key work requirement. With no forklifts available back in those days, carting 50kg bags of potatoes and loading truckloads of watermelons was all part of the job description.

    From Italy

    By 1962 after bringing all his family out from Italy, Fred opened his very first shop “Fred’s Fruit Market” at the age of 22. From 1962-1967 the family worked in the store & also lived above the shop.

    Fresh

    We provide seasonal fresh fruit and vegetables. We have all kinds of fruit and vegetables.
